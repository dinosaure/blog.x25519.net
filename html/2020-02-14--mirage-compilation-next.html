Into my previous article, I just showed up some internals about the MirageOS compilation. However, the story is a bit complex. This article wants to go further and it will describe much more details.

A story about targets

MirageOS can compile an unikernel to 3 targets:

  • the UNIX target
  • the Solo5 target
  • the Xen target

For each, we need to a special link step to produce the right ELF file.
